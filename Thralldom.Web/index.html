About Thralldom

Thralldom is a 3D game inspired by the novel 'Under the Yoke' by the Bulgarian author Ivan Vazov. It tells the story of the April Uprising of 1876, the insurrection that kick-started the bloody fight for Bulgarian indepedence.

The game runs entirely in the browser. You can play it right away by clicking here or at the top of the page.

Unfortunately, we only fully support Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox. Internet Explorer (v11) is on the way.

The Game

Look trough the eyes of Boycho Ognyanov, a revolutioner that has just escaped the heavily-guarded prison of Diarbekir as he returns to his homeland, seeking ways to overthrow the Ottoman government and finally restore the long-lost indepedence of the Bulgarian people.
